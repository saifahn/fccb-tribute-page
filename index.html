Porter Robinson

DJ, Producer

Porter Weston Robinson is an American DJ, record producer and musician from North Carolina. He is currently ranked number 90 on DJ Mag's Top 100 DJs list for 2016.


He began producing at age 12, and in 2011 released his EP Spitfire under Skrillex's record label OWLSA. Since then, he has released a number of tracks that have taken the #1 overall chart position on Beatport, as well as releasing an album with his own personal style.
